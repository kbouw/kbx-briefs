### 2. Notes on Pope Leo XIV's encyclical on AI

Pope Leo XIV's new encyclical 'Magnifica Humanitas' provides unusually clear guidance on AI ethics, directly addressing LLM interpretability challenges, algorithmic bias, environmental costs, and the risks of delegating human decisions to systems that lack 'compassion, mercy, forgiveness.' The Vatican explicitly frames this as their response to the AI revolution, paralleling Leo XIII's 1891 labor encyclical during the first industrial revolution.
