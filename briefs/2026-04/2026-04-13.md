## Design

### 1. That’s a Skill Issue

Jan Miksovsky's Web Origami project exemplifies human-centered design by treating user confusion as a design failure rather than a skill issue, contrasting with AI proponents who put the burden on users to learn the technology's language. This approach prevents creating a priesthood of expert users and keeps technology serving people as they actually are.
