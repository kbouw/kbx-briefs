# Daily Intelligence Brief — March 24, 2026

## AI/ML

### 1. Streaming experts

Dan Woods' streaming experts technique is proving viable for running massive MoE models on consumer hardware—Kimi K2.5 (1T parameters) now runs in 96GB RAM on M2 Max, and Qwen3.5-397B even runs on iPhone at 0.6 tokens/sec by streaming expert weights from SSD. This could democratize access to trillion-parameter models without requiring datacenter hardware.

**Source:** [Simon Willison's Weblog](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Mar/24/streaming-experts/#atom-everything)

### 2. Quoting David Abram

David Abram argues that LLMs can't solve the hardest parts of software development—understanding systems, debugging complex issues, designing scalable architectures, and making strategic decisions—because they lack true system understanding, context retention, and decision-making capability. The real valuable work remains knowing what should exist and why, not just typing code.
