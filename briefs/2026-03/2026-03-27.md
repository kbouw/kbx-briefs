### 2. Quantization from the ground up

Sam Rose's interactive essay reveals that LLM quantization from 16-bit to 8-bit carries almost no quality penalty (~100%), while 16-bit to 4-bit maintains ~90% quality despite using quarter precision. Critical finding: rare "super weight" outlier values are so important that removing even one can cause complete gibberish output, requiring special preservation in production quantization schemes.

**Source:** [Simon Willison's Weblog](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Mar/26/quantization-from-the-ground-up/#atom-everything)
