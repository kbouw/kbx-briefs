### 1. 🔴 The pressure

Daniel Stenberg reports that AI-assisted security research has increased curl vulnerability reports to 4-5x 2024 levels (over one per day), creating unsustainable workload pressure on maintainers despite most findings being low-medium severity. This highlights the emerging challenge of AI amplifying security research faster than open source projects can handle responsibly.
