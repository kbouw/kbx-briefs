## AI/ML

### 1. Granite 4.1 3B SVG Pelican Gallery

Testing IBM's new Apache 2.0 Granite 4.1 3B model across 21 GGUF quantized variants (1.2GB to 6.34GB) on SVG generation revealed no clear quality-to-size correlation—all variants performed poorly at drawing pelicans on bicycles, suggesting the base model lacks visual generation capabilities regardless of quantization level.
