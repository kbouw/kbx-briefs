### 2. The Zig project's rationale for their firm anti-AI contribution policy

Zig's VP of Community Loris Cro explains their strict LLM ban using "contributor poker" theory—maintainers invest in growing long-term contributors, not just landing code, so LLM-assisted PRs waste review time since they don't develop trustworthy human contributors. This policy blocked Bun from upstreaming a 4x performance improvement to Zig's compiler after their Anthropic acquisition.

**Source:** [Simon Willison's Weblog](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Apr/30/zig-anti-ai/#atom-everything)
