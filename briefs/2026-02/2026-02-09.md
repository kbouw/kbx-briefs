### 1. AI makes the easy part easier and the hard part harder

AI tools excel at automating routine, well-defined tasks but actually increase complexity in creative and ambiguous work by introducing new coordination overhead and decision-making layers. This creates a productivity paradox where AI simultaneously accelerates simple workflows while making strategic and creative work more challenging to manage.
