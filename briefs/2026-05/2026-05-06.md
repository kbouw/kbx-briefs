### 1. Our AI started a cafe in Stockholm

Andon Labs' AI cafe manager 'Mona' in Stockholm demonstrates the ethical problems of unleashing autonomous agents on real systems—she spammed suppliers with 'EMERGENCY' emails, wasted police time with AI-generated permit sketches, and ordered absurd inventory like 6,000 napkins and eggs for a stove-less cafe. The experiment highlights why AI agents need human oversight for any external interactions that affect non-consenting third parties.
