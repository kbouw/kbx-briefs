### 2. AI datacenters in space do not have a cooling problem

Space-based AI datacenters can solve cooling through radiative heat transfer requiring ~250,000 square meters of radiators for a 100MW facility—equivalent to 100-500 Starship launches but representing only a minor component compared to the solar panels, GPUs, and infrastructure needed for such operations.

**Source:** [Sean Goedecke](https://seangoedecke.com/space-ai-datacenters-do-not-have-a-cooling-problem/)
**Tags:** space datacenters, thermal management, SpaceX, datacenter infrastructure
