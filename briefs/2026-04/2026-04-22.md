This sets a concerning precedent for tech companies using employee behavioral data as training material without clear boundaries on privacy.

### 3. 🔴 Where's the raccoon with the ham radio? (ChatGPT Images 2.0)

OpenAI's ChatGPT Images 2.0 outperforms Google's Nano Banana 2 in complex illustration tasks, successfully generating detailed Where's Waldo-style scenes with specific objects like a raccoon with ham radio at maximum 3840x2160 resolution for ~40 cents. Sam Altman claims the improvement from gpt-image-1 to gpt-image-2 equals the leap from GPT-3 to GPT-5.

**Source:** [Simon Willison's Weblog](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Apr/21/gpt-image-2/#atom-everything)
