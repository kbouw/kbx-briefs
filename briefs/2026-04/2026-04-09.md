### 1. 🔴 Meta's new model is Muse Spark, and meta.ai chat has some interesting tools

Meta released Muse Spark, their first model since Llama 4, competitive with Opus 4.6 and GPT 5.4, featuring 16 integrated tools including Code Interpreter, web browsing, Meta content search across Instagram/Facebook/Threads, and Claude Artifacts-style HTML rendering. The toolset reveals Meta's strategy to create a comprehensive AI workspace that can access and manipulate content across their entire ecosystem.
