### 1. Autoresearching Apple's "LLM in a Flash" to run Qwen 397B locally

Dan Woods implemented Apple's "LLM in a Flash" technique to run Qwen3.5-397B (209GB) on a 48GB M3 Max at 5.5 tokens/sec by streaming 2-bit quantized expert weights from SSD while keeping 5.5GB of critical components in RAM. Using Claude to auto-generate 90 experimental variants, he reduced active experts from 10 to 4 per token with claimed minimal quality loss.
