## AI/ML

### 1. 🔴 Google AI Edge Gallery

Google released the first official vendor app for running local LLMs on iPhone—AI Edge Gallery runs Gemma 4 models (2.54GB E2B) with image analysis, audio transcription, and eight interactive tool-calling demos. This marks a significant shift toward official mobile deployment of local models, though conversations remain ephemeral without permanent logs.

### 2. Show HN: I built a tiny LLM to demystify how language models work

Developer open-sourced a minimal 9M parameter transformer implementation in just 130 lines of PyTorch that trains in 5 minutes on free Colab T4 GPUs, demonstrating that understanding LLM fundamentals doesn't require massive models or compute resources.
