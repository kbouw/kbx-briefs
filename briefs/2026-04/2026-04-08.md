### 3. 🔴 OpenAI says its new model GPT-2 is too dangerous to release (2019)

OpenAI withheld the full GPT-2 model in 2019, claiming it was too dangerous for public release due to potential misuse for generating convincing fake text. This marked a pivotal shift toward staged AI releases and sparked industry debate about AI safety versus open research.
