# Daily Intelligence Brief — May 23, 2026

## Industry

### 1. The memory shortage is causing a repricing of consumer electronics

AI's demand for high-bandwidth memory (HBM) is consuming 20% of memory manufacturers' wafer capacity by 2026 versus just 2% previously, with each HBM gigabyte requiring 3x more wafer capacity than consumer RAM. This shift will significantly increase prices for consumer electronics over the next several years, already impacting sub-$100 smartphones in developing markets.
