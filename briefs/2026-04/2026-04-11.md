### 1. ChatGPT voice mode is a weaker model

OpenAI's Advanced Voice Mode runs on the older GPT-4o model (April 2024 cutoff) while their premium coding models use much more capable systems, creating a misleading capability gap where the most accessible AI interface is actually the weakest. This happens because coding domains have verifiable reward functions for RL training and higher B2B value, concentrating OpenAI's best talent on those models.
