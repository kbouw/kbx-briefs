### 5. From SGD to Muon: An Incremental Tutorial (Fable-5 vs Opus 4.8)

This tutorial builds up the Muon optimizer by showing how matrix-aware preconditioning can dramatically improve on Adam's diagonal limitations—Shampoo uses Kronecker factorization to reduce memory from O(d²) to O(m²+n²), while Muon combines this with cheap orthogonalization via Newton-Schulz iterations for momentum plus matrix-structured updates.
