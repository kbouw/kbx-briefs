### 2. I don't know if my job will still exist in ten years

A staff engineer argues that unlike previous automation scares, AI will genuinely eliminate software engineering jobs within a decade—not through a programming plateau where AI writes code but humans maintain it, but because AI agents are rapidly improving at code maintenance and becoming "usually faster and sometimes more insightful" than human engineers.
