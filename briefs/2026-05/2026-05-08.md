### 2. 🔴 Behind the Scenes Hardening Firefox with Claude Mythos Preview

Mozilla used Claude Mythos preview to find hundreds of Firefox vulnerabilities, jumping from 20-30 security fixes per month to 423 in April 2025 by combining improved AI capabilities with sophisticated prompting techniques that filter signal from noise. The approach discovered decades-old bugs including a 20-year-old XSLT vulnerability, proving AI can now generate high-quality security research at scale.
