### 1. Quoting Matt Webb

Matt Webb argues that while AI agents can brute-force solve coding problems by "grinding them into dust" with massive token usage, the real value comes from architectural thinking—building great libraries with interfaces that make the right approach the easiest path. He's spending less time looking at code and more time on architecture while "vibing" (his term for AI-assisted development).
